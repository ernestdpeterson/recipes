3.5 oz pecans
6 oz all purpose flour
4 tbl butter
2 oz ice watter
.5 tsp kosher salt

Directions:
Pulse pecans in food processor until mealy
Add flour and salt
Pulse to combine
Add cold butter
Pulse to combine
Add cold watter
Pulse 3 times to bring mass together
Place in plastic bag and compress into a disk
Refridgerate for .5 hours
Roll into thin sheet (about an eighth of an inch thick)
Place in pie pan and add filling
